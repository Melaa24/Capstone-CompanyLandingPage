Flying Feathers!

Here at Mugherini Farms the chickens are molting. This means they are losing and regrowing all their feathers. This occurs every year as the days start getting short. While they are molting, our chickens stop laying eggs.

About Our Farm

History

In the year 2018, nestled by Freeses Pond in Deerfield NH, 4 baby chickens were born. They were named Tina, Gerry (hens) and The Bobs (roosters). As the flock got older, they moved outside to their verry own chicken coop. For us Mugherini's, it was love at first site. The following year we added 4 more chickens. Every year we add a different bread and the flock just keeps growing!
